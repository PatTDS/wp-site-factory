# Precast Concrete Services Industry - Competitor Research

## Major Players in Australia

### Premier Cranes & Rigging (Melbourne)
- Specializes in precast concrete panel erection, tilt up panels, precast beams
- Uses CAD software for lift studies and Sketch Up for 3D project views
- Provides propping recommendations for complex installations
- Active since 2008, serving government and large-scale commercial projects

### BSL Group (Melbourne)
- 30+ years experience in precast concrete panel installation
- Serves builders, architects, and infrastructure companies
- Team of fully qualified riggers with extensive training
- Focus on quality craftsmanship

### Rankin Steel and Rigging (Gold Coast/Queensland)
- Specializes in precast and tilt-up concrete panel installation
- Advanced crane and rigging capabilities
- Services Gold Coast, Brisbane, and Northern Rivers

### RAR Group (Canberra/National)
- Notable projects: National Portrait Gallery, Sydney Opera House, Pluto LNG Plant, Australian War Memorial
- Complete installations of architectural precast including post-installation works
- Supply and erection of concrete precast panels

### Sventek Cranes & Rigging (Melbourne)
- Safety-first approach to precast panel erection
- Expertise in job set-out, drilling, chemsetting dowel bars, welding
- State-of-the-art machinery, cranes, and rigging equipment

### Urban Cranes (Western Australia)
- Comprehensive lift plans, sequence drawings, SWMS and risk-assessment
- Total installation packages: crane hire, safety equipment, personnel
- Finishing services: joint sealing and grouting

## Common Service Offerings

### Core Services
1. **Precast Panel Erection** - Lifting and positioning panels
2. **Tilt-Up Panel Installation** - On-site tilt-up construction
3. **Crane Coordination** - Working with crane operators
4. **Lift Planning** - Sequence drawings and analysis
5. **Propping** - Temporary support systems
6. **Joint Sealing** - Waterproofing between panels
7. **Grouting** - Base and connection grouting

### Post-Installation Services
- Concrete repairs
- Skincoating and finishing
- Crack injection
- Coverplate installation

## Differentiators & Trust Signals

### Commonly Highlighted
- Years of experience (10-30+ years common)
- Major project portfolio
- Safety certifications and protocols
- ISO certifications
- Partnership with major builders
- Comprehensive lift planning capabilities
- End-to-end project support

### Website Messaging Themes
- "Start to finish" support
- Safety-first approach
- Precision and quality
- Reliability and partnership
- Experience with major brands/builders

## Pricing Models

- Project-based quotes (most common)
- Per linear metre (joint sealing)
- Per square metre (repairs, skincoating)
- Daily/hourly rates (labour)

## Target Customers

- Commercial builders
- Industrial developers
- Warehouse/logistics construction
- Retail construction
- Precasters (panel manufacturers)
- General contractors
