# Testimonials Section Best Practices (General)

## Purpose

Testimonials provide social proof by:
1. **Validating claims** - Real customers confirm quality
2. **Building trust** - Others like me had good experiences
3. **Overcoming objections** - Address concerns through stories
4. **Adding credibility** - Third-party endorsement

## Key Principles

### Effective Testimonials Include

- **Specific results** - Not just "great service"
- **Customer name** - Full name adds credibility
- **Photo** - Real face increases trust 30%+
- **Location/Role** - Relatable context
- **Date/Timeframe** - Recent is better

### Testimonial Quality Hierarchy

1. **Video testimonials** - Most powerful
2. **Photo + detailed story** - Very effective
3. **Photo + quote** - Good
4. **Name + quote** - Acceptable
5. **Anonymous quote** - Weak (avoid)

### Integration with Review Platforms

- Google Reviews widget
- Facebook Reviews
- Industry-specific (HomeAdvisor, Houzz, etc.)
- Star ratings visible

## What Works

### Content Elements

- Specific outcomes ("saved us $5,000")
- Emotional language ("finally a plumber we trust")
- Problem-solution format
- Before/after when applicable
- Response to common objections

### Layout Options

- Carousel of testimonial cards
- Grid layout
- Featured testimonial with supporting quotes
- Video testimonials gallery
- Embedded Google Reviews widget

### Trust Enhancers

- Star ratings (4.8/5.0)
- Number of reviews ("200+ 5-star reviews")
- Review platform logos
- "Verified Customer" badges

## What to Avoid

- Fake-sounding testimonials
- Stock photos for "customers"
- Generic praise without specifics
- All testimonials from same time period
- Missing attribution
- Overly polished/corporate language

## Formula Templates

### Standard Testimonial Card

```
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

"[Specific outcome or experience]. [Emotional statement about the result].
[Would recommend statement]."

— [Full Name], [Location]
   [Context: Homeowner / Business Type / Project Type]
   [Date or "Customer since 2020"]

[Photo of customer]
```

### Detailed Testimonial

```
## [Headline summarizing result]

[Photo of customer or project]

"[Full testimonial - 3-4 sentences covering problem, solution, outcome]"

**[Full Name]** | [Location]
[Project type] | [Date]

✓ [Specific metric or outcome]
✓ [Another specific result]
```

### Example

```
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

"After three different roofers gave us runaround quotes, RoofPro came out
the same day, gave us a clear price, and had the leak fixed by dinner.
No mess, no stress. Finally a roofing company that does what they say."

— Sarah M., Parramatta
   Homeowner, Roof Leak Repair
   October 2024

[Photo of Sarah]
```

### Review Summary Block

```
## What Our Customers Say

"[Short quote 1]" — Name
"[Short quote 2]" — Name
"[Short quote 3]" — Name

[Read All Reviews Button]
```

## Testimonial Collection

### How to Request

- Follow up 1-2 weeks after service
- Make it easy (provide questions to answer)
- Offer incentive if appropriate
- Ask for Google review as alternative

### Questions to Ask

1. What problem were you trying to solve?
2. Why did you choose us?
3. What was the result?
4. Would you recommend us?
5. Anything else you'd like to add?
