# Project Discovery Questions

Use these questions when starting a new website project to gather all necessary information.

---

## Phase 1: Company Basics

### Essential Questions

1. **Company Name**
   - What is the official company name?
   - Any variations or short names?

2. **Industry/Sector**
   - What industry does the company operate in?
   - Are there specific sub-sectors?

3. **Domain**
   - Do you have a domain name?
   - If not, what domains are you considering?

4. **Location**
   - City:
   - State/Province:
   - Country:
   - Full address:

5. **Contact Information**
   - Primary phone number:
   - Primary email address:
   - WhatsApp number (if different):
   - Business hours:

---

## Phase 2: Brand Identity

### Visual Identity

1. **Logo**
   - Do you have an existing logo? (provide file)
   - What formats do you have? (SVG, PNG, JPG)
   - Do you need logo design assistance?

2. **Brand Colors**
   - Primary color: (hex code if known)
   - Secondary color: (hex code if known)
   - Any colors to avoid?

3. **Typography Preference**
   - Modern/Clean (sans-serif)
   - Traditional/Elegant (serif)
   - Technical/Mono (monospace)
   - No preference

4. **Tone of Voice**
   - Professional and formal
   - Friendly and approachable
   - Technical and expert
   - Casual and relaxed

5. **Competitors/Inspiration**
   - List 2-3 competitor websites you like
   - What do you like about them?
   - What would you do differently?

---

## Phase 3: Services/Products

### Core Offerings

1. **Services/Products List**
   - List all main services or products
   - Which are the most important (top 3)?
   - Are there seasonal or temporary offerings?

2. **Unique Selling Proposition**
   - What makes you different from competitors?
   - Why should customers choose you?

3. **Target Audience**
   - Who are your primary customers?
   - Demographics (age, location, profession)?
   - What problems do you solve for them?

4. **Pricing**
   - Will pricing be displayed on the website?
   - Fixed prices or "request quote"?
   - Any promotional offers to highlight?

---

## Phase 4: Content

### Available Content

1. **About the Company**
   - Brief company history
   - Mission statement
   - Vision statement
   - Company values

2. **Team**
   - Do you want to feature team members?
   - How many team members?
   - What information for each? (name, title, photo, bio)

3. **Testimonials**
   - Do you have customer testimonials?
   - How many?
   - Do you have permission to use them?

4. **Case Studies/Portfolio**
   - Do you have completed projects to showcase?
   - Photos available?
   - Client permission to display?

5. **Blog/News**
   - Will you publish regular content?
   - How often?
   - Who will write it?

---

## Phase 5: Features

### Required Features

1. **Contact Form**
   - Simple contact form?
   - Multiple contact forms (different purposes)?
   - Required fields?

2. **Newsletter**
   - Do you want newsletter signup?
   - What email platform? (Mailchimp, etc.)

3. **Social Media**
   - Facebook:
   - Instagram:
   - LinkedIn:
   - WhatsApp:
   - YouTube:
   - TikTok:

4. **Maps Integration**
   - Show business location on map?
   - Multiple locations?

5. **Special Features**
   - Online booking/scheduling?
   - E-commerce/store?
   - Customer portal/login?
   - Live chat?
   - FAQ section?
   - Search functionality?

---

## Phase 6: Technical

### Hosting & Infrastructure

1. **Hosting Provider**
   - Do you have existing hosting?
   - Provider name:
   - FTP/SFTP credentials available?

2. **Domain Registrar**
   - Where is domain registered?
   - Do you have DNS access?

3. **Email**
   - Do you have business email?
   - Provider (Google Workspace, Outlook, etc.)?
   - Need email setup assistance?

4. **SSL Certificate**
   - Existing SSL?
   - Need SSL setup?

5. **Backup Requirements**
   - How often should we backup?
   - Where should backups be stored?

---

## Phase 7: Timeline & Budget

### Project Expectations

1. **Timeline**
   - Ideal launch date?
   - Any hard deadlines?
   - Milestones needed?

2. **Budget**
   - Budget range for development?
   - Ongoing maintenance budget?

3. **Priorities**
   - What's most important? (design, speed, features, SEO)
   - What can be added later (phase 2)?

---

## Quick Checklist Summary

After discovery, confirm you have:

- [ ] Company name and location
- [ ] Contact information
- [ ] Logo file (or need for design)
- [ ] Brand colors
- [ ] Services/products list
- [ ] About company content
- [ ] Team member information (if needed)
- [ ] Testimonials (if available)
- [ ] Social media links
- [ ] Hosting access
- [ ] Domain access
- [ ] Timeline expectations
